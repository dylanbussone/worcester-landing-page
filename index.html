Weather Guidelines

Summer

We have light landscaping service scheduled every other week. Please be sure not to leave any personal items outside when you’re away, as the landscapers will need unobstructed access to the yard.

If the outdoor temperature exceeds 85°F, we kindly ask that you open the window in the pantry before using the dryer. This helps prevent the fire alarm from being triggered due to excessive heat buildup.


Winter

During snowstorms, the city may require that cars be removed from the street. In these cases, other tenants may temporarily park down the driveway. Don’t be alarmed if you see additional cars, but please let me know if any are parked in a way that prevents you from exiting.

If a snow emergency is declared, I’ll notify you so you can move any street-parked vehicles to avoid being towed. Please note: we are not responsible for any towing fees incurred for parking on the street during a snow emergency. If your car is towed, you can call the city’s 311 helpline or the non-emergency line at (508) 799-8606 for assistance.

Walkways, driveways, and parking areas can become slippery in winter conditions. Please use caution and wear appropriate footwear. While we do our best to clear and treat these areas before and during your stay, weather can change quickly. We may need your help during your visit. A shovel and ice melt are available by the front door in the hallway for your convenience
